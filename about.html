About Aasman

Understanding our mission and vision

What is Aasman?

Aasman, meaning "Sky" in Urdu, represents the limitless potential of Pakistan's space program and the boundless capabilities of Pakistani women in STEM fields. This digital platform documents Pakistan's space exploration journey while celebrating the remarkable contributions of Pakistani women who are reshaping the future of science and technology.

We believe that just as the sky knows no bounds, neither should the dreams and achievements of women pursuing careers in STEM. Through comprehensive databases, inspiring stories, and educational resources, Aasman serves as a bridge between Pakistan's space aspirations and the urgent need for greater women's representation in science and technology.

About This Project

Aasman is a semester project created by Aamina, Maryam and Waasie, students at the Institute of Space and Technology (IST), as part of our Applications of ICT course for Fall 2025 semester.

This website combines our passion for space exploration with our belief in empowering women in STEM. This is an academic project, but we hope it inspires real change and serves as a valuable resource for students, professionals, and anyone interested in Pakistan's space program and women's advancement in science and technology.
